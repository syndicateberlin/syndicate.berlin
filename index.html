The
Syndicate

We’re a friendly community of creative types based in Berlin. Our members include designers, developers, writers, illustrators, and founders.

How to join?

New members are inducted into our Slack community every day, with priority given to referrals from existing members.

What is Slack?

Slack is a cross-platform messaging app for teams, where conversations are organised into channels. You can access Slack with your browser, or by installing an app on your desktop or smartphone.

Who are we?
